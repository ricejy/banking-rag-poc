Connecting Global Financial Institutions and Asian Businesses

Our Global Financial Institutions team leverages on our strong regional franchise to deliver solutions specially tailored to your business needs.

Broad geographical footprint

OCBC Bank’s strong presence in Asia is well-established with full banking licenses in our key markets of Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, China and Hong Kong SAR. Our extensive local networks include close to 500 branches across our subsidiaries of OCBC Malaysia, OCBC NISP and OCBC Wing Hang, complemented by OCBC Bank’s branches in Thailand, Vietnam, Myanmar, Hong Kong SAR, Taiwan, Korea, Japan, Australia and the United Kingdom.

Customised solutions and local expertise

With the full suite of services across cash management, trade finance, treasury, capital markets and financing options, approach your coverage team for tailored solutions to both your needs and your clients’ needs in Asia.
