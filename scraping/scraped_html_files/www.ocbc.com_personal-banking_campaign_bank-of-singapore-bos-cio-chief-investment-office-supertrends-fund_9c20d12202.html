Reasons to invest

Access Bank of Singapore’s CIO expertise

Benefit from the expertise and research capabilities of Bank of Singapore’s global Chief Investment Office (CIO), with over 15 years of proven investment track record*.

*Bank of Singapore Discretionary Portfolio Management (SG)’s investment track record.

A regular income stream with growth potential

This global multi-asset strategy is optimised to provide monthly payouts from fixed income while providing investors potential capital appreciation through global equities.

Focus on quality

A rigorous research-based approach to identifying quality companies within equities and fixed income, aiming to find resilient business models and investments based on robust fundamentals.

Capitalise on supertrends

Supertrends1 are structural trends that could shape the investment landscape over a longer term. This fund aims to achieve long-term returns by aligning structural global trends with rigorous investment insights.

1 Refers to the Supertrends mentioned in the Supertrends Report published by Bank of Singapore. Supertrends are structural trends that may involve consideration of issues such as geopolitics, macro policies, and investment trends. Such Supertrends are not exclusive, may be subject to change, and their attributes might evolve over time at the discretion of the Sub-manager.
