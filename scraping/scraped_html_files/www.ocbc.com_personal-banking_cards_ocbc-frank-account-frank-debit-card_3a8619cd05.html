Important notice

For existing OCBC FRANK Account and OCBC FRANK Debit Card customers

From 1 October 2025, when you charge at least S$100 to an OCBC FRANK Debit Card (linked to an OCBC FRANK Account) each month, you will earn either extra interest of 1.00% a year OR extra cashback of 1.00%, whichever is higher. This is on top of the base interest of 0.60% a year on your account balance and base cashback of 0.30% on your debit card spending. For details, refer to our FAQ.

  • Half-yearly CPF statement

Card replacement fee

Lose a card? We get it. That is why we will waive the card replacement fee for the first two cards. After that, it will cost S$32.70 (including GST) to replace your card.

Fee for foreign currency transactions

Non-USD foreign currency transactions are first converted to USD before being converted to SGD based on the rate determined by the respective card associations.

All foreign currency transactions will incur a currency conversion charge imposed by the respective card associations (1% of the transaction amount) and a bank administrative fee (2.25%).

Currency conversion fee

An additional fee will be charged on all Visa and MasterCard transactions made in SGD and processed overseas (including online transactions). This includes but is not limited to: (i) Any Visa or MasterCard retail transaction presented in foreign currency that you choose to convert to and pay in SGD via dynamic currency conversion; and (ii) Any online retail card transaction charged to Visa or MasterCard cards in SGD on websites based overseas. The additional fee charged is 2.8% of the converted SGD amount for each Visa and MasterCard transaction.
