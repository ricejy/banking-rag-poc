Why you will love this

Pay a low one-time processing fee

Who can apply

OCBC credit card members (OCBC NXT Card, OCBC Great Eastern Cashflo Credit Card or Debt Consolidation Card are not eligible to apply for PayLite)

Apply online

Split your credit card transactions of at least $50 each into fixed, interest-free monthly instalments (EIR* from 9.50% p.a.). Apply easily via the OCBC app now.

FEATURES

how to apply via THE OCBC APP

1

After logging in to the OCBC app, select “Cards”, your card, and “PayLite”.

2

Select the card used to make the transaction(s) you wish to put on PayLite. Only cards eligible for PayLite and their transactions will be shown.

3

Select the transaction(s) you wish to put on PayLite (an indicative summary will be shown at the bottom) and tap 'Next'.

4

Select the instalment period and tap 'Next'

5

Review the repayment schedule and tap 'Submit'.

If your application is successful, the instalment details will be shown in your card transaction history on the next day.

More questions and answers
How does PayLite work?

You can split your eligible OCBC Credit Card purchase of $50 and above, into interest-free (EIR from 9.5% p.a.), fixed monthly instalments. Choose between a tenor of 3, 6, 12 months and pay a one-time processing fee of as low as 3%.

What are the eligible transactions for PayLite?

PayLite is only applicable for retail transactions. This excludes Cash Advance, Balance Transfer, Bank Fees and Charges, 0% IPP transactions, Cash-on-Instalments transactions, and such other transactions as OCBC may determine.

Are all OCBC Credit Cards eligible for PayLite?

No, OCBC NXT Credit Card, OCBC GE Cashflo Credit Card and Debit Consolidation Card are not eligible for PayLite.

Is there a maximum amount that I can apply PayLite for?

No, there is no maximum amount applicable.
