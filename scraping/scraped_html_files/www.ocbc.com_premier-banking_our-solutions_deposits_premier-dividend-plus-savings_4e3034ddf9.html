    Deposit Insurance Scheme

    Singapore dollar deposits of non-bank depositors and monies and deposits denominated in Singapore dollars under the Supplementary Retirement Scheme are insured by the Singapore Deposit Insurance Corporation, for up to 100,000 SGD in aggregate per depositor per Scheme member by law. Monies and deposits denominated in Singapore dollars under the CPF Investment Scheme and CPF Retirement Sum Scheme are aggregated and separately insured up to 100,000 SGD for each depositor per Scheme member. Foreign currency deposits, dual currency investments, structured deposits and other investment products are not insured.

    FAQS
    Common questions
    How do I earn the various types of interest?

    You can earn base interest and bonus interest every month:

    1. Base interest
      This interest is accrued daily based on your account’s end-day balance. You will receive this at the end of the month.
    2. Bonus interest
      You will earn two types of bonus interest on your account’s average daily balance. After you meet the following requirements, you will earn bonus interest on the 1st working day of the following month:
      • ‘No withdrawal’ bonus of 1.00% a year: Make no withdrawals in a month
      • ‘No withdrawal and save’ bonus of 0.55% a year: Make no withdrawals and deposit 3,000 SGD in your account in a month
    When do I need to deposit at least 3,000 SGD in my account to qualify for the ‘No withdrawal and save’ bonus?

    The value date of your deposit must fall within the month you wish to earn bonus interest for. You may make a deposit via any method, such as a recurring FAST payment. If you make the funds transfer on a non-working day (e.g. Sunday or a public holiday) via FAST or PayNow, the value date will be the next working day.

    Is there a limit to the bonus interest I can earn?

    No. So long as you meet the bonus interest requirements, bonus interest will be calculated based on your entire account balance.

    What will happen if I make a withdrawal in a month?

    You will earn base interest.
