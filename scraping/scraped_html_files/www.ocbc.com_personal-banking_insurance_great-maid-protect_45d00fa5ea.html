GREAT MAID PROTECT

Give your domestic helper the protection she needs

How this will benefit you

Up to S$90,000 in coverage for hospital and surgical expenses

Up to S$60,000 in coverage for accidental death or permanent disability

Up to S$50,000 in coverage against claims made by a third party due to injury or death caused by your domestic helper’s negligence

Apply now
 

Limited-time offer: 20% off your Great Maid Protect plan Find out more

GREAT Maid Protect provides comprehensive coverage for your domestic helper.

Features and Benefits

Get up to S$90,000 in hospital and surgical expenses

Choose from four comprehensive plans that reimburse hospitalisation and surgical expenses*, including overseas medical emergencies and communicable diseases like COVID-19.

*Hospitalisation & surgical expense section is subject to pro-ration factor and co-payment factor if applicable.

Be protected from accidents, whether big or small

Get your domestic helper up to S$60,000 in coverage for accidental death or permanent disability. If she is in an accident, she will also get up to S$3,000 in coverage for outpatient medical expenses incurred at a general practitioner or polyclinic.

Safeguard your finances from unexpected events

Get up to S$50,000 in coverage against claims made by a third party due to injury or death caused by your domestic helper’s negligence.

Get up to S$10,000 in reimbursement when you send your helper back

Receive up to S$10,000 as a reimbursement if you need to send your domestic helper back to her home country in the case of death or permanent disablement due to an accident or illness.

FAQs
Common questions about GREAT Maid Protect
More questions and answers
If my maid is hospitalised due to Tuberculosis will the hospital and surgical expenses incurred be covered under my GREAT Maid Protect?

Yes, the hospital and surgical expenses will extend to cover communicable diseases which include Influenza A flu virus (H1N1), Hand Foot and Mouth Disease (HFMD), Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS) and Tuberculosis.

How does the Insurance Guarantee work?

Upon purchasing GREAT Maid Protect Silver, Gold or Platinum, a Letter of Guarantee will be issued on the employer's behalf to Ministry of Manpower (MOM). If MOM demands payment under the guarantee, we will pay to MOM and subsequently recover the amount from the employer. However, purchasing the Bond Protector1 will free the employer from this obligation to pay us. This benefit under Bond Protector is only applicable if the forfeiture was due to no fault of the employer.

1Subject to an excess of S$250.

Why does Maid insurance have a 14 or 26 months insurance and bond when MOM only issues 12 or 24 months work permit?

This is an MOM requirement. MOM requires all insurance and bond to have an additional 2 months of cover so that in any event / reason the maid overstays in Singapore after her work permit has expired, she is still covered under the insurance.

Will my helper be covered for Outpatient medical treatment?

Yes, you are covered for outpatient treatments up to the sum insured provided that they are a result of injury.
