How this will benefit you

Receive up to S$500,000 on accidental death and total permanent disablement

Reimbursement for medical expenses due to 16 infectious diseases including Zika and Hand, Foot and Mouth Disease

Who can apply

Anyone between 18 and 50 years old, renewable up to age 75
