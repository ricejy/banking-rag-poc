FAQs
Common questions
How can I link my debit card to my Global Savings Account?

Each debit card can only be linked to one Global Savings Account. Once you link your debit card to a Global Savings Account, you can use it to make purchases in the foreign currencies held in the account.

Learn more about linking a debit card to your Global Savings Account.

Is there a minimum amount required before I can create an Auto-Execute Order and how long is the order valid for?

The minimum amount required to create an Auto-Execute Order is 500.00 of the currency you wish to sell. The validity of each Auto-Execute Order is between 1 day and 30 days. After the 30th day, the Auto-Execute Order will expire if your preferred rate is not met.

Can I buy FX in a currency which I do not have in my Global Savings Account?

Yes you can. The new currency would be added into your Global Savings Account.

How can I buy or sell foreign currency through the OCBC app?

Follow our step-by-step guide on how to buy or sell foreign currency through the OCBC app.

Attractive real-time exchange rates

The online foreign exchange function is only available between 8am on Monday and 11.59pm on Friday.

On weekends, you will see an estimated foreign exchange rate when you set up any foreign exchange transaction involving SGD or USD. If you decide to make the transaction, we will process it the next working day at the prevailing foreign exchange rates.

Get your preferred FX rates on the go

Rate Alerts and Auto-Execute Orders are only available on the OCBC Digital app.

Rate Alerts: Do note that there will be no currency exchanged. A Rate Alert will be valid for 30 days after creation. If the rate is not met, the Rate Alert will expire. You may renew it afterwards.

Auto-Execute Orders: If you wish to exchange currencies at a preferred rate, you can create an Auto-Execute Order for a predetermined amount to be exchanged automatically when your preferred rate is met.
