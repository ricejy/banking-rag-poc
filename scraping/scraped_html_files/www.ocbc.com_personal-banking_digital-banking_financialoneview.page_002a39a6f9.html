About OCBC Financial OneView and SGFINDEX

Enabled by SGFinDex (Singapore Financial Data Exchange), OCBC Financial OneView consolidates all your financial information securely, so that you can track all your accounts in one place.

SGFinDex is a joint initiative by Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS), and the Smart Nation and Digital Government Group (SNDGG). This gives individuals access to financial information held across different government agencies and financial institutions.

Manage all your finances in one place

Looking for better ways to track your finances? Consolidate all your financial information from 7 participating banks (OCBC Bank, Citibank, DBS/POSB, HSBC, Maybank, Standard Chartered Bank, UOB), 7 participating insurers (AIA, HSBC Life, Great Eastern Life, Manulife Singapore, Income, Prudential Assurance Company Singapore and Singlife) – SGX CDP, and selected government agencies such as CPF, IRAS, and HDB.

View your consolidated financial data securely

Just like how you have entrusted us with safeguarding your money, your data will be securely protected with stringent security measures (e.g. end-to-end encryption). Singpass is also required to log in to SGFinDex to retrieve your data.

Get personalised insights beyond just data summary

Use insights personalised for you to understand where you’re at and where you want to be. Let us get you there so you can save more, earn more, and spend wisely.

Achieve your financial wellness with guided journeys

Stay on top of your finances without the need for complex spreadsheets or third party apps. Use our simple and intuitive tools to help you make better-informed decisions towards achieving your goals.
