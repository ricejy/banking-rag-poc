      FAQs
      Common questions
      When does OCBC release its financial results?

      Our quarterly results are released within 45 days of the close of the quarter and audited results within 2 months of the financial year ending on 31 December. Please view our Financial Calendar for results announcement information.

      Where can I get a copy of OCBC's financial results?

      Past financial results can be found in the Financial Results section.

      Where can I get a copy of the annual report?

      Softcopies may be downloaded from the Annual report and AGM section.

      Where can I view OCBC's results briefing?

      Webcast link for the latest half year/full year results briefing can be found in the Financial Results section.
