More questions and answers
What is a digital card?

A digital card is the debit or credit card that you can easily access from the OCBC Business Mobile App or OCBC Velocity after the approval of your application.

The digital card will have the same card number and expiry date as the physical card that you will receive. The only difference between the digital card and physical card is that of the CVV.

Follow this guide to learn how to access your digital debit card details and start using it. Please ensure that your OCBC Business Mobile App is updated.

Can we make cash withdrawals using the Business Debit Card from ATMs outside of Singapore?

Yes, you may make cash withdrawals using your Business Debit Card from ATMs when you are overseas. However, the ATM must be able to accept the 6-digit PIN.  Please match the card association logo on your Business Debit Card to the ATM, for example: MasterCard-Cirrus.

How do I add my Business Debit Card to my Mobile Wallet?

To add your Business Debit Card to your mobile wallet, please toggle the “Mobile Wallets” setting in the OCBC Business Mobile App. Refer below to see the step-by-step guide.

OCBC Business Mobile App
1. Log in to OCBC Business Mobile App
2. Go to Accounts and select the linked account
3. Tap on the Business Debit Card
4. Turn on Mobile Wallets feature
5. Go to your mobile wallet and add the Business Debit Card.

How do I increase the daily limit for NETS/ATM withdrawal and/or spending limit for my Business Debit Card? What is the maximum limit that I can increase for my spending and withdrawal?

You may submit a request through the OCBC Business Debit Card Maintenance Form. 

Maximum limit for spending: $50,000
Maximum limit for NETS/ATM withdrawal: $5,000

Please note that it may take up to 7 business days to process your request

How do I reset my Business Debit Card pin?

You may reset your PIN via OCBC Velocity / the OCBC Business Mobile App or submit a request through the OCBC Business Debit Card Maintenance Form.

Please note that it may take up to 7 business days to process your request.

OCBC PictureCard service - Image Guidelines

These Image Guidelines may be varied by OCBC at any time. OCBC will not accept images that contain any of the following:

  • trademarks, logos, slogans, company names, get-up, copyrighted material or brands of any third party
  • advertising or promotional material, or branded products
  • celebrities, actors, musicians, athletes, cartoons or public figures
  • works of art
  • phone numbers, addresses, URL addresses, account or PIN numbers
  • provocative or sexual material
  • violent or other offensive, obscene or indecent material
  • nudity or semi-nudity
  • bigotry, racism, hatred, profanity or obscenities
  • alcohol, tobacco, gambling, firearms or pharmaceutical products
  • images that show illegal or anti-social behaviour
  • culturally or socially insensitive images
  • images that display the opinions or beliefs of political groups, religious groups or socially unacceptable groups (e.g. political images, neo-Nazi images, gangs)
  • libellous or defamatory content, or content which is otherwise in violation of any rules, regulations or laws to which the images are subject
  • content not acceptable for persons under 18 years old
  • images that may result in non-acceptance or other problems at point of sale.
  • such other images or contents as may be deemed inappropriate or unacceptable by OCBC.
