Common questions about the OCBC INFINITY Cashback Card
More questions and answers
How much cashback can I earn?

You can earn 1.6% per S$1 spent on the OCBC INFINITY Cashback Card in both SGD and foreign currency.

Is there a minimum spending requirement?

There is no minimum spending required to start earning your cashback! You can start earning your cashback from the first dollar of spend.

Is there a limit to the amount of cashback I can earn a month?

There is no limit on the amount of cashback you can earn monthly, so you enjoy more cashback as you spend more!

What types of transactions will I not earn cashback on?

The usual spend exclusions apply, please refer to the Terms and Conditions Governing the OCBC INFINITY Cashback Card for the full list of exclusions.
