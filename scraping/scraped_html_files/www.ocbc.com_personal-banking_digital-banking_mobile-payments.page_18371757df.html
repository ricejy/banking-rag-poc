MOBILE PAYMENTS
  • Apple Wallet
  • Samsung Wallet
  • Google Wallet™

1

Open the Wallet app and tap '+' to add a card.

2

Tap ‘Debit or Credit Card’ and follow the instructions shown to fill in details of the card you wish to add.

3

Tap ‘Verify with OCBC’ or ‘Verify with App’ to open the OCBC app and authorise the request by tapping ‘Confirm’.

4

Return to the Wallet app to finish adding your card. For added convenience, set the card as your default card for payment.

1

Open the Samsung Wallet app and tap ‘+’ to add a card.

2

Tap ‘Payment cards’ and follow the instruction shown to fill in the details of the card you wish to add.

3

Tap ‘Go to bank website’ (if you are adding a Mastercard) or ‘Card issuer web verification’ (if you are adding a Visa card).

4

When you receive a push notification from OCBC, tap it to open the OCBC app and authorise the request by tapping ‘Confirm’.

5

Return to the Samsung Wallet app to finish adding your card. For added convenience, set the card as your default card for payment.

1

Open the Google Wallet™ app and tap ‘+’ or ‘+ Add to Wallet’, then ‘Payment card’.

2

Tap ‘New credit or debit card’ and follow the instructions shown to fill in the details of the card you wish to add.

3

Tap ‘Sign in to bank website’.

4

When you receive a push notification from OCBC, tap it to open the OCBC app and authorise the request by tapping ‘Confirm’.

5

Return to the Google Wallet™ app to finish adding your card. For added convenience, set the card as your default card for payment.
