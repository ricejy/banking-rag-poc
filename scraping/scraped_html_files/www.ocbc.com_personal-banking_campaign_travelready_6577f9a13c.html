Get travel-ready with OCBC

Spend less effort managing your travels and more time enjoying them with our suite of products and services.

TRAVEL EASY FROM START TO FINISH

Manage your pre-trip needs with ease

Exclusive travel deals
Make the most of your trip with our travel deals. Get discounts on flights, hotel accommodation, activities and more – on platforms like Agoda, Trip.com and Klook – when you pay using OCBC credit/debit cards.

Global Savings Account
Convert major currencies like AUD, CAD, CHF, EUR, GBP, HKD, JPY, NZD, USD at great rates before your trip – no admin fees or fall-below charges. Get real-time alerts and lock in your desired rates automatically on the OCBC app.

Explorer Travel Insurance
Get Explorer Travel Insurance early and be covered 30 days before your trip. Receive up to S$700,000 in overseas medical coverage; our extensive coverage includes trip cancellations, flight delays, baggage loss and more.

For convenience, buy the policy via the OCBC app – we will pre-fill any information that we have in our records and give you the option of using your OCBC account to pay.

PayLite
Split large travel bookings – like flights, hotel accommodation and car rentals – into smaller, interest-free monthly instalments (EIR from 9.50% p.a.). This means you can pay over time and save on stress.

Skip the queue at the money changer

Scan & Pay
Go cashless during your travels – simply scan and pay using the OCBC app. Make secure payments via Alipay+, UnionPay, DuitNow QR and more at merchants across 40 destinations, including China, Malaysia and Japan.

Enjoy competitive exchange rates and zero additional fees or transaction charges.

Global Savings Account
Easily withdraw cash abroad while saving on foreign currency fees. Simply use your OCBC debit card to withdraw AUD, CAD, CHF, EUR, GBP, HKD, JPY, NZD, USD from your Global Savings Account at overseas ATMs.

OCBC 90°N Card
Be rewarded when you charge foreign currency transactions to the OCBC 90°N Card. Earn unlimited Miles that never expire at 2.1 Miles for every S$1 spent in foreign currency and 1.3 Miles for every S$1 spent.

OCBC VOYAGE Card
Make the most of your overseas spending. Earn VOYAGE Miles when you charge selected foreign currency transactions to the OCBC VOYAGE Card – and use these Miles to redeem your next flight out on any airline!

Plus, boost your travels with exclusive perks like complimentary access to airport lounges and limo transfer services.

Manage your holiday expenses

PayLite
Got a little carried away with shopping, eating or seeing sights during the trip? Put your transactions of at least S$100 on PayLite to pay them in fixed instalments (EIR from 9.50%).
