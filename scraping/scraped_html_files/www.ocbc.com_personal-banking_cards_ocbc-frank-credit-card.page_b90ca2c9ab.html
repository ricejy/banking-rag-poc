Is a FavePay transaction considered an online or in-store contactless payment?

A FavePay transaction made via the Fave app will be classified as an online transaction, since it is made through the Internet via an app.

Only Apple Pay, Samsung Pay and Google Pay™ transactions will be classified as in-store contactless payments.

Which transactions do not count towards the minimum monthly spending and cashback calculations?

Transactions under certain Merchant Category Codes (MCCs) will not count towards minimum monthly spending or cashback calculations. Examples include top-ups to e-wallets like Grab, YouTrip and Singtel Dash; tax payments; and insurance premium payments made to financial institutions.
