    How to activate your DragonPass membership

    Gain access to an expanded network of over 1,400 airport lounges with a complimentary DragonPass membership – exclusive to customers who have the OCBC Premier Visa Infinite Card.

    To activate your membership:

    1. Download the DragonPass app via the Apple App Store or Google Play Store
    2. Launch the app and tap ‘Sign up’
    3. Tap ‘Activate Account Benefit’
    4. Enter the membership number and activation code we sent to your email address in our records. Then, tap ‘Continue’
    5. Enter your email address and tap ‘Next’
    6. Provide all required details, agree to the Privacy Policy and tap ‘Next’
    7. Enter the verification code that you will receive via email before tapping ‘Continue’
    8. Create your password and tap ‘Done’ to activate the membership!

    * Applied for your OCBC Premier Visa Infinite Credit Card between the 1st and 15th of the month? If your application has been approved, you will receive your membership number and activation code via email between the 22nd and 25th of that month.

    Applied for your Card between the 16th and last day of the month? If your application has been approved, you will receive the details between the 7th and 10th of the following month.
