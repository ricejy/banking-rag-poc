In the second issue, Little Money Master: Digital Banking 101 – Stay Safe and Smart, we feature comics inspired by the film Zootopia. In the amazing city of Zootopia, anyone can be anything, though it is not always easy. Judy Hopps accomplishes her dream of becoming a cop, but she ends up in a police force with buffalo, lions, rhinos, polar bears and elephants—who don’t see her as quite their equal. When she gets the chance to work on a real case, she knows she has to prove herself. The problem is, she needs the help of a fast-talking, scam-artist fox called Nick Wilde. But can a rabbit really trust a fox?
