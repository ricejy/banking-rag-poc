OCBC Chinese New Year Deposit Promotion 2026 - Other CASA

 

Hi there!

Please complete the form below to participate in the OCBC Chinese New Year Deposit Promotion 2026 - Other CASA.

Here's how:

  1. Deposit fresh funds of at least S$50,000 in an eligible account within the Campaign Qualifying Period (i.e. by 31 January 2026), up to a maximum of S$3,000,000.
  2. Select an eligible account.
  3. Confirm your details.
  4. Submit your registration form for the promotion below by 31 January 2026.
  5. If you are successful, your funds will be earmarked for 88 days, starting from the date of your eligible deposits.

If eligible, you will be entitled to receive the following Bonus Interest on your fresh funds amount:

One (1) Bonus Interest of 1.75% p.a. on your fresh funds deposited

Promotion Details

Please take note:

  • The sign up period is from 1 January 2026 to 31 January 2026.
  • “Fresh Funds” refers to funds in the form of non-OCBC cheques/ cashier’s orders/ demand drafts, funds that are not transferred from any existing OCBC accounts, and funds that are not withdrawn within 30 days before or during the Promotion Period and re-deposited (whether in part or in full amounts withdrawn) into the Eligible Account during the Promotion Period for purposes of qualifying for the Promotion
  • The money you use for this promotion will be withheld for 88 days, and is counted from the date of fresh funds deposited.
  • Bonus interest rate is only applicable to the fresh funds amount that is deposited into the account and will only be paid for the duration of the earmark.
  • Only monies deposited into the Eligible Accounts as stated above are eligible for the promotion.
  • Please note that funds secured under Money Lock cannot be earmarked.


When will I know I am eligible?

  • Once you are successfully enrolled, your funds will be set aside for 88 days from when the deposit was made.
  • We will send an SMS notifying you of the outcome of your application.

When will I receive the bonus interests?

  • Bonus interest will be credited by 31 May 2026.

Will I receive the bonus interest if I withdraw the funds before the expiry of the earmark?

  • No, you will not receive any bonus interest if you withdraw during the earmark period.
  • In the event that we have credited the bonus interest, we reserve the right to claw back the bonus interest that was paid out.

Deposit Insurance Scheme: Singapore dollar deposits of non-bank depositors and monies and deposits denominated in Singapore dollars under the Supplementary Retirement Scheme are insured by the Singapore Deposit Insurance Corporation, for up to S$100,000 in aggregate per depositor per Scheme member by law. Monies and deposits denominated in Singapore dollars under the CPF Investment Scheme and CPF Retirement Sum Scheme are aggregated and separately insured up to S$100,000 for each depositor per Scheme member. Foreign currency deposits, dual currency investments, structured deposits and other investment products are not insured.
