Supporting your business growth in Thailand and beyond

Operating from Bangkok, our local team offers expert insights and guidance on expanding domestically or overseas

Overview

OCBC’s presence in Thailand has commenced since 2 July 1909 with the establishment of a branch in Bangkok. OCBC Bangkok branch is now under the supervision of Bank of Thailand and Anti-Money Laundering Office. We have obtained Foreign Bank branch license, FX License and Payment license to provide a range of banking products and services including Deposits, Loans, Trade Finance, Foreign Exchange, Derivative products as well as Remittance services.

Our capabilities

Combined with our mature portfolio of services as well as our long and reliable reputation in Thailand, capitalise on our deep local market knowledge to support your business expansion. We offer sustainable and efficient business solutions for network customers, large Thai corporates and financial institutions (FI).

We provide deposits, loans, trade finance, foreign exchange as well as interest rate swaps to various industries such as Energy, Petrochemicals & Refining, Food & Beverages, Automotive, Trading, Real Estate and FI. Driving the demand for digital, we have introduced Business Internet Banking (OCBC Velocity) and the Business Mobile Banking application to enhance efficiency, speed and agility in digital transactions for our customers. Now, you can easily manage your business accounts, check balances, initiate transactions safely and securely, all at your convenience.
