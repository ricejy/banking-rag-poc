Overview

OCBC New York Agency (NYA) was established in April 1981 while OCBC Los Angeles Agency (LAA) began as a representative office in October 1987 before upgrading to Agency status in July 1989.

The ‘Agency’ label comes from how banks headquartered outside of the United States are licensed to operate. It restricts the range of services the offices may offer, but in all other aspects, both offices are branches of OCBC Bank. NYA is supervised by both the New York State Department of Financial Services and the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, while LAA is supervised by the California Department of Financial Protection and Innovation and the Federal Reserve Bank of San Francisco.

Both NYA and LAA primarily provide wholesale banking services such as commercial lending and trade finance to network customers. NYA also engages in large deposit taking. Both offices do not offer retail services.

Our capabilities

Capitalise on our deep local market knowledge to support your business expansion. We offer sustainable and efficient business solutions, for wholesale banking needs, including loans and advances, deposits, trade finance and remittance services.

Commercial lending

To support businesses to achieve business growth, improve cash flow and cover expenses, OCBC USA provides flexible finance and borrowing options. Our specialists will customise a financing solution that meets your business needs.

Trade services

Protect your interests and manage trade risk effectively with our payment assurances and trade solutions. Strengthen customer relationships by extending payment terms while increasing your own working capital.

Deposit Taking (NYA only)

Our deposit services in NYA allows you to safely stow your deposits in your business account. Manage your cash flows more efficiently with our business banking solutions.
