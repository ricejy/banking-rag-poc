About Sushi Moka

At Sushi Moka, you will find yourself in the hands of a master chef with experience in 2 Michelin-starred restaurants in Japan. Master Ueda Toshihiro's delectable sushi creations reflect his 42 years of experience in Osaka, Tokyo and Singapore.

Reserve now

Specialty

‘Sushi Moka’ means ‘peach blossom’ in Japanese. Here, you will find fresh ingredients, flown in daily from Japan. Moka's sushi omakase brings seasonal seafood to the next level, and features rice from Niigata that has been seasoned with a secret blend of rice vinegars. On occasion, you may find yourself being served a creation inspired by the Japanese 'Mokas'.
